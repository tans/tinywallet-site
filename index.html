How and Why

  1. 1. Layer Pocket, we build a Pocket Layer. Any transactions upon Pocket Layer do not need to write to Blockchain.so no gas fee is required.
    For example, when a user signup, we could mint the user some coin or NFT, but no need to write to the blockchain. until the user collects enough coins or sells the NFTS, user could withdraw to his wallet.
  2. 2. Embed Internal Wallet, users do not need to keep their privatekey[12 worlds]. the internal wallet makes users control their wallet with their passwords, and recoverable.
  3. 3. Why do we hold wallets and assets for users? Cause as a brand or company, we should complete the user's experience, and have the responsibility to make the product usable for most people not make a product for hackers.
